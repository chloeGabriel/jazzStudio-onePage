Who we are

We're jazz and cat lovers! Charlie, Joe and Angie created the label in 2013 for young jazz artists. Musicians, singer, producers, we're all collaborate together to promote the new generation of jazz.

Listen our productions

We produce all sort of Jazz music. From old school type to Latin, Bebop, Smooth, Nu-Smooth and Avant-Jazz, we have an amazing music palette of creation from our artists. Our jazz music can be used for movies, advertising, concert, radio station, podcast and more.

Our services

Part of our benefits goes to shelters! Besides of our love for music, we love animals and we want to protect them. Being part of Jazz Cat Studio is a way of contributing financially to shelters, supporting the people working there everyday for free who take care of our furry friends.

Want to join us?

Musicians, singers, producers and passionated people, everyone can join the team! If you would like to be a member of our studio, give us a shot! Fill the form in our contact section. We'll be happy to hear more about you.
